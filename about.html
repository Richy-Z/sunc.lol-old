Our Mission At sUNC, our mission is to create a safer and more transparent environment for the scripting community. We strive to empower users with the tools they need to identify and avoid fake or misleading executors. By providing a reliable way to distinguish between genuine and fraudulent software, we aim to protect users from scams and ensure that they have access to authentic and trustworthy scripting solutions. Our commitment is to promote honesty and integrity in the community, helping users make better, informed decisions with confidence.
